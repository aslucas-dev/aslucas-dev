### 🛠 Engineering Portfolio & Mindset

I specialize in bridging the gap between hardware constraints and complex software requirements. My work is characterized by **Hardware Abstraction** through direct register manipulation (CMSIS), ensuring **low-latency firmware** for mission-critical applications.

#### 🛰 Featured Expertise: Control & Navigation
*   **Extended Kalman Filter (EKF):** Implementation of high-order algorithms for **Sensor Fusion** (IMU Yaw + GNSS), providing robust orientation and positioning for autonomous systems.
*   **Interrupt-Driven Architectures:** Professional-grade drivers (e.g., STM32F0/F1 UART) featuring circular buffers, atomic operations, and optimized ISR management.
*   **Industrial Communication & Protocols:** Extensive experience with **ModBus (RTU/ASCII), CAN BUS, DMX, NMEA, ETHERNET**, and Long-Range telemetry (**LoRa - RFM95W**).
*   **Control Systems:** Development, implementation, and **tuning of PID algorithms**, focusing on performance optimization and stability for diverse control applications.
